## Design System

This document outlines the core design principles and token values for our digital products.

### Theme

**Color Mode**: light
**Headline Font**: plusJakartaSans
**Body Font**: beVietnamPro
**Label Font**: beVietnamPro
**Roundedness**: 3 (Maximum, pill-shaped)
**Spacing**: 2 (Normal)

### Color Palette

Our brand leverages a vibrant color palette, with a primary focus on distinct, functional hues.

- **Primary Color**: `#006837` - The brand's most distinctive chromatic color, suitable for buttons, CTAs, and key interactive elements.
- **Secondary Color**: `#F9A825` - A supporting color for less prominent UI elements, chips, and secondary actions.
- **Tertiary Color**: `#FF7043` - An additional accent color for highlights, badges, or decorative elements.
